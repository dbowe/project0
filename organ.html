Third (Fourth?) Instrument: The Pipe Organ

When my youngest child entered preschool and I was looking for something more to do, I decided to take pipe organ lessons just because and ultimately became the assistant director of music at our church, playing this king of instruments on a regular basis.

I am currently Music Director at Pilgrim Congregational Church in Lexington, MA where I play a small but delightful instrument.
