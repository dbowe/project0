My Second Instrument: The Clarinet

I picked up the clarinet as a fifth grade student in the Waltham Public Schools. After two years of group lessons, I studied with William Cabral and Anthony Fulginetti over the remainder of my years in the Waltham Schools.

My clarinet playing is limited these days as I don't have the time or interest in maintaining an embrochure, but I yearly participated in the Parent Teacher Student (PTS) band sponsored by the Belmont Schools. Several years ago I participated in On Stage at Symphony, a program sponsored by the Boston Symphony Orchestra whereby amateur musicians in the local area rehearse and perform a concert on the stage at Symphony Hall. The above photo was taken as a publicity for that event.
